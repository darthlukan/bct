About Me

Sith, Linux Lover, Programmer, Freedom Fighter, Husband, Father. Not necessarily in that order.

I am a California native who's been living and working in Stavanger, Norway for the last two and a half years as an Integration Developer. Currently, I am seeking new opportunities in Software Development within Europe because the thought of not writing code for a living is depressing.

I grew up on Linux, quite literally, and can't remember the last time I willingly used another operating system. While there is definitely room for improvement, I enjoy the Open Source and FOSS communities and try to contribute as much as I can to both, whether it be through code or in helping new and seasoned users troubleshoot their problems in IRC.

My wife and I met in High School, dated then, and have now been married for almost 14 years. We have two wonderful daughters who give us many reasons to smile everyday and some fish that keep us entertained when we're not gaming, watching movies, or enjoying the Norwegian outdoors.
